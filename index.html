SAP Cloud Products

lexibility, speed, transparency, efficiency and process control – the demands on production and the supply chain are continually growing, but companies with end-to-end processes are well positioned to face this challenge.

Three important processes in SAP are:

Procure-to-Pay or Purchase-to-Pay (P2P) maps the procurement of goods, goods receipt, and the receipt and payment of supplier invoices. Plan-to-Manufacture (P2M) covers the processes from planning to production. Order-to-Cash (O2C or OTC) implements the processes from ordering through to goods receipt and payment by the customer.

Ensuring goods reach customers on time requires a transparent network of end-to-end processes for procurement, order handling, production, logistics and inventory management. valantic offers companies integrated solutions and services to digitize their processes end-to-end, from the initial development of a strategy to implementing state-of-the-art solutions for supply chain management and logistics.

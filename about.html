About Us

Intelligent end-to-end processes along the entire value chain are fundamental to successful businesses. As a SAP-PartnerEdge-Gold-Partner and SAP Cloud Focus Partner, valantic has extensive skills in optimizing all forms of business processes. Our SAP consultants and developers support several hundred prominent customers in every area – from strategy development and implementation to SAP system operating and license sales. And this, for over 30 years.

Our SAP Services customers include numerous DAX-40 companies as well as leading medium-sized enterprises in Germany, Austria and Switzerland, including: Bardusch, BASF, Bizerba, Ehrmann, Fuchs Petrolub, Hero, Hochland, Lindt, Melitta, Playmobil, Raiffeisen Waren-Zentrale (RWZ), Rehau, Schleich, Siemens Mobility, Springer Nature, thyssenkrupp Materials Processing Europe, Toyota and the Theo Müller Group.

Our Partners

We support industrial companies in achieving their SDG goals, especially in the area of CO2 reduction. Through efficient IT use, especially in the SAP environment, we create the greatest possible transparency along the supply chain and thus promote the synergy effects of the circular economy. Our membership in respact offers the ideal platform for networking and mutual exchange with like-minded companies in order to continue to think about and promote Corporate Social Responsibility.
